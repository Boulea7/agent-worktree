# Research Policy

## Purpose

Research is first-class here, but raw research should not overwhelm public documentation.

## Public Research

Commit:

- synthesized conclusions
- decision-driving comparisons
- future direction summaries

## Local Research

Keep local and ignored:

- raw dumps
- chat transcripts
- scratch notes
- copied article text
- personal rankings

## Evidence Rule

AI-generated concept material can inform direction, but it must not be treated as proof without validation.
